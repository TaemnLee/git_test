Image Explanation

JPGs

JPG images are good for photos.

  1. It contains large color palettes without too much increase of size
  2. Transparent pixels are not allowed

GIFs

GIFs are good for animations.

  1. It is good for animations
  2. In trade off, it has limited palettes
  3. There are only binary transparent pixel options

PNGs

PNGs are good for diagrams, icons, or logos.

  1. It is good for animations
  2. In trade off, it has limited palettes
  3. It has flexible transparency degree

SVGs

GIFs are good for animations.

  1. Unlike other pixel based image formats above, it has vector-based graphics format.
  2. It can be scaled up to any dimension
  3. You might need image editor for better compatibility across browsers
